# Sonido Chamuco
By Zira Reyesna

## Location: DK Kitchen, room 169
## Date: 02.07.2026, 19:00 – 22:00

Mexican ‘Sonidero’ is a communal digital live-set experience centered on Latin American rhythms. Emerging in working-class neighborhoods during the mid-20th century, sonideros transformed music and live-set into a tool for connection and belonging.
Through dance, live mixing, and visuals, this project explores Latin American identities within migratory contexts.
