# Angewandte Screenings

## Location: VZA 7, Auditorium
## Time: daily

For the first time this year, the auditorium will be transformed into a cinema during the festival, screening video works and short films from various departments. Visitors can take a break in the cool auditorium and watch the film programme on a continuous loop. 
Angewandte Screenings developed in collaboration with Christoph Höschele (TransArts).


Participating artists DK: Sei Jeung, Zita Kayser, Iris Kienzl, Ryta Kulyk, Malpractice, David Obradović, Anton Paievski, Andreas Rippl, Herner Werzog
